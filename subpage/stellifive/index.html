Values

Core beliefs that drive Stellifi

Scalability

We look for companies providing innovative solutions that reduce barriers to scalability in an otherwise antiquated and fragmented industry.

Tech Fatigue

As operators ourselves, we understand staff resistance to learning new tech tools and look for products that prioritize ease of use and reduce friction to training.

Capital Efficiency

As fellow entrepreneurs, we believe in creating sustainable businesses founded by ambitious & resourceful founders who understand the importance of deploying capital efficiently.

Focus

We believe that large scale solutions are solved one problem at a time and support open API systems rather than data silos.

ESG

We promote solutions that think proactively & responsibly about our environmental future.

Team

We are a like-minded group of Entrepreneurs, Operators, and Investors that share common values and goals.
